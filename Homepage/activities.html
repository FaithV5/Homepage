Exciting Activities in Mabini

Scuba Diving

Explore the vibrant coral reefs and marine life of Anilao, the diving capital of the Philippines.

Snorkeling

Enjoy the shallow reefs and crystal-clear waters perfect for snorkeling adventures.

Hiking

Climb Mt. Gulugod Baboy for a panoramic view of Batangas Bay and Verde Island Passage.

Kayaking

Take a boat ride to explore nearby islands, hidden lagoons, and untouched beaches.
