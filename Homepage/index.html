Welcome to Mabini

Nestled along the Batangas coastline, Mabini is known as the "Diving Capital of the Philippines". With its vibrant coral reefs, crystal clear waters, and scenic mountain views, it’s the perfect destination for adventurers, families, and nature lovers alike.
